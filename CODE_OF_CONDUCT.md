# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experience levels
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer on GitHub. All reports will be reviewed and investigated.
